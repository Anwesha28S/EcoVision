Why Application for waste-classification?

Awareness Matters

The world generates at least 3.5 million tons of waste per day and this number is still increasing day by day. Manual segregation of these wastes requires huge manpower and time, on the other hand, this also results in human error due to which the whole process is hampered. That's why we need to be aware about waste.

This app helps users to classify waste into 4 different categories, namely Hazardous, Non-recyclable, Organic and Recyclable. The recycling process for these materials is complex, and it is incredibly difficult to sort them once they reach a recycling factory.

The future scope of this is to integrate, Computer Vision and IOT for classification at the source along with at the waste segregation firms. Currently, 70% of waste is not recycled simply because it was never properly classified at the source.
